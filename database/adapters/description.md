Adapters bridge the gap between controllers and pixels, providing proper connections, level shifting, and sometimes additional features like fusing or status LEDs.

These boards are especially useful for:

- **Breakout connections** - Converting compact headers to screw terminals
- **Level shifting** - Built-in voltage translation for reliable signals
- **Power distribution** - Fused outputs and power injection points
- **Mounting** - DIN rail or case-friendly form factors
