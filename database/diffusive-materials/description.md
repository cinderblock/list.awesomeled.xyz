Diffusive materials soften the appearance of individual LEDs, creating smooth gradients and professional-looking light effects.
Without diffusion, you see distinct dots; with it, you see flowing ribbons of color.

Material properties to consider:

- **Light transmission** - How much brightness you lose through the material
- **Color rendition** - Whether colors shift or remain accurate
- **Flexibility** - Rigid channels vs bendable silicone tubing
- **Form factor** - Flat panels, tubes, domes, or custom shapes
