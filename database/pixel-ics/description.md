Pixel ICs are the driver chips inside addressable LEDs.
Understanding these chips helps you choose the right pixels for your project and troubleshoot compatibility issues.

Some ICs are found in multiple pixel products, while others are proprietary.
Key differences include:

- **PWM frequency** - Affects flicker visibility (especially when filming)
- **Data protocol** - WS2811-style single-wire vs SPI-based clocked protocols
- **Channel count** - 3-channel (RGB) vs 4-channel (RGBW)
- **Refresh rate** - How quickly the IC can update its output
