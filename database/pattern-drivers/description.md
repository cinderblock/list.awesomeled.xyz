Pattern driver software creates and sends the pixel data that makes your LEDs come alive.
From simple color cycling to complex synchronized shows, these applications are where the creative magic happens.

Options range from free open-source tools to professional-grade commercial software:

- **Live performance** - Real-time control with music reactivity
- **Sequence design** - Timeline-based animation editors
- **Visualization** - Preview your design before installation
- **Protocol support** - Art-Net, sACN, DDP, DMX output options
